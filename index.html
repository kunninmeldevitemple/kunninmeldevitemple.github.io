Welcome

Welcome to the official website of Kiliroor Kunninmel Devi Temple.

Kiliroor Kunninmel Devi Temple (കിളിരൂർ കുന്നിന്‍മേല്‍ ദേവി ക്ഷേത്രം) is a serene and spiritually vibrant temple located in Kiliroor, near Thiruvarpu in Kottayam district, Kerala. Resting atop a gentle hill, this centuries-old shrine is dedicated to Goddess Parvati, revered here as the supreme mother who blesses devotees with prosperity, protection, and peace. The temple’s elevated location offers a tranquil atmosphere, making it a much-loved destination for worshippers and visitors alike.
