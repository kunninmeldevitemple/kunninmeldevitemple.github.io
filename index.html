A Divine Retreat at Kiliroor Kunninmel

Tucked away in the serene landscapes of Kottayam, Kiliroor Kunninmel Devi Temple (കിളിരൂർ കുന്നിന്‍മേല്‍ ദേവി ക്ഷേത്രം) is a spiritual beacon for devotees. Perched on a gentle hill, this ancient shrine is dedicated to Goddess Parvati, who is revered as the divine mother and a source of peace, prosperity, and protection.

This website provides a glimpse into the temple's history, traditions, and festivals. We hope it assists you in your journey to this sacred place.

About the Temple

The Kiliroor Kunninmel Devi Temple is steeped in history and folklore, with its origins believed to date back several centuries. The presiding deity, Goddess Parvati, is worshipped in her tranquil form, embodying maternal love and divine power. The temple's architecture showcases traditional Kerala style, with its intricate woodwork and a serene sanctum sanctorum.
